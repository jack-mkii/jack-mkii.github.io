Welcome to Raid Timers!


Raid Timers is a utility for use during trials* in The Elder Scrolls Online. Consoles lack anything similar to PC's add-ons, and thus have no easy way to time raids --- this utility allows you to do just that. Need to know where your team can cut down for progress, titles, or records? Raid Timers can help!

Each trial has been split into its respective boss fights, with timers for each of the add phases in between. There is also a timer available for "idle time". During a prog, there's usually a decent amount of down-time between encounters as teams do things like discussing strategies, practicing gear-swaps, and more. You can use the idle timer to track this, allowing your encounters to be timed more accurately and giving you a more fine-grained breakdown of where your time is spent.


Functionality



Starting a timer will pause any active timers, and begin timing the new encounter.



Stop the current timer.



Reset the current timer to 0. You can use this to reset the timing of a section on a group wipe, for example.




Pause any active timers, and start the idle timer. On mobile, this will skip the carousel to the 'Idle time' slide.



Reset all timers to 0.

*Not all ESO trials are currently implemented. The timers available are for Halls of Fabrication, Cloudrest, Sunspire, and Kyne's Aegis.
